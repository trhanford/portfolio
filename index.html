About Me

Hands-on mechanical + electrical builder focused on tidy, serviceable systems and clear documentation.

I design and build integrated mechanical and electrical systems: CAD packaging, harness design, cooling, controls integration (ECU/CAN), and validation. I like neat looms, serviceability, and a well-named netlist.

Core Skills

  • Siemens NX / Creo Parametric
  • Harness design, PDM, sensors & relays
  • MATLAB, Python, LTspice
  • Thermals: fan/shroud, ECU temp triggers

How I work

  • Plan → build → instrument → test → iterate
  • Write test plans and capture results
  • Document for handoff (prints, schematics, BOMs)
