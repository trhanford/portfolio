About Us

Quality, Innovation, and Passion in Every Custom Build

Our Story

Tiny Customs is a proud subsidiary of Summit Metal Products, located in Batavia, Illinois. Founded with an unyielding passion for automotive excellence, our journey began with the creation of award‐winning custom cars. We combine decades of experience in innovative design and precision engineering to transform classic visions into modern masterpieces.

With a dedication to quality and an eye for detail, we ensure that every custom build exceeds expectations. Our legacy is built on a commitment to the highest standards of craftsmanship, advanced engineering, and timeless style.

Meet Our Founders

Tristan

Tristan’s passion for custom cars and innovative engineering has led him to design vehicles that not only perform exceptionally but also embody exquisite style. His expertise in mechanical systems drives our award-winning builds.
